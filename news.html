Paper on two new cyanobacterial species (Laspinema) using population genomics approach published!

June 10, 2024

Our paper on the new cryptic species Laspinema olomoucense and Laspinema palackyanum was published in the Journal of Phycology. In this paper, we apply population genomics to define boundaries between the lineages and propose two new cryptic species from Olomouc. Check out the paper here.
